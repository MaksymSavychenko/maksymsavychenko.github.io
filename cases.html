Selected Strategic Programs

Complex risk architecture and large-scale insurance structures

Aviation Risk Programs — Passenger & Cargo Fleet

Structuring and placement of one of the largest aviation insurance and reinsurance programs in Ukraine, including coverage of war risks on specialized international markets.

The program covered passenger airlines and cargo fleet operations (including “Aerosvit” and “Antonov”) and at certain stages represented up to 10% of the company’s total revenue.

Focus — portfolio stability, international placement strategy and sustainable risk economics.

Renewable Energy Portfolio — DTEK

Structuring and reinsurance placement of a large-scale renewable energy portfolio — one of the most complex programs on the market in terms of risk concentration, catastrophic exposure and international underwriting requirements.

Integrated risk modeling, layered capacity allocation and strategic placement on international markets.
